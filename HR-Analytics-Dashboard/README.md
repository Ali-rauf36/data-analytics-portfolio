# HR Analytics Dashboard

**Tool:** Power BI Desktop  
**Dataset:** IBM HR Employee Attrition · 1,470 employees

## Dashboard Overview
Interactive HR dashboard analyzing employee attrition patterns 
across departments, age groups, job satisfaction levels, and income.

## Key Findings
- Overall attrition rate is 16.12% — above healthy industry average of 10-15%
- Sales department has the highest attrition rate
- Employees under 25 leave at the highest rate — early career mobility pattern
- Clear inverse relationship between job satisfaction and attrition
- Research Scientists have the lowest avg income ($) — potential attrition driver in R&D
- Gender shows minimal difference in attrition (53.5% male vs 46.5% female)
